Bill Notice

EA issues bill notices to consumers regarding their electricity usage and the corresponding charges. The notice includes details such as the billing period, meter reading, consumption, tariff rates, and the amount to be paid. It serves as a reminder for consumers to settle their electricity bills.This bill notice serves as a reminder of your electricity consumption and the corresponding charges for the billing period of [billing period]." "Your meter reading for this period is [meter reading], indicating a total consumption of [consumption] units." "The tariff rates applicable to your electricity usage are as follows: [mention the applicable tariff rates]." "The total amount due for this billing period is [amount due]. Please ensure payment by the due date to avoid any late payment charges or disconnection of service." "You can make the payment through various convenient methods, including online payment, bank transfer, or visiting your nearest NEA office. Please refer to the back of this notice for detailed payment instructions." "In case of any discrepancies or queries related to this bill, kindly contact our customer support helpline at [customer support contact number]." "Please retain this notice for your records and reference." "Thank you for your prompt payment and cooperation in settling your electricity bills on time." Remember,

About Us

The Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) is responsible for several key functions related to the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity in Nepal. Here are some of the primary tasks and activities undertaken by NEA:Electricity Generation: NEA is involved in the generation of electricity through its own hydropower projects and thermal power plants.Transmission and Grid Operation: NEA operates and maintains the national transmission grid, which connects power plants to substations and major load centers across the country.
