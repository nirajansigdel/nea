About Us

The Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) is responsible for several key functions related to the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity in Nepal. Here are some of the primary tasks and activities undertaken by NEA:Electricity Generation: NEA is involved in the generation of electricity through its own hydropower projects and thermal power plants.Transmission and Grid Operation: NEA operates and maintains the national transmission grid, which connects power plants to substations and major load centers across the country.
