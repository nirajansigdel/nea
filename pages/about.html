what we do?

The Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) is responsible for several key functions related to the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity in Nepal. Here are some of the primary tasks and activities undertaken by NEA: Electricity Generation: NEA is involved in the generation of electricity through its own hydropower projects and thermal power plants. It also collaborates with independent power producers (IPPs) who generate electricity and supply it to NEA. The authority is responsible for planning, developing, and managing the generation capacity in the country.
