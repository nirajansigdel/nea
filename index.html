Nea Information

The Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) is the government-owned utility responsible for generating, transmitting, and distributing electricity in Nepal. Established in 1985, the NEA plays a crucial role in Nepal's energy sector and is tasked with ensuring reliable and affordable electricity supply to meet the country's growing demand.

Our Anniversary

The 31th anniversary of the Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) would be a significant milestone for the organization. It is an occasion to reflect on NEA's accomplishments, growth, and contributions to the electricity sector in Nepal over the years. The anniversary provides an opportunity to celebrate the achievements, recognize the efforts of NEA staff, and acknowledge the organization's role in the country's development. Commemorative Ceremonies: NEA may hold special ceremonies or gatherings to celebrate the anniversary. This can involve speeches, presentations, and the participation of government officials, NEA management, employees, and stakeholders. Award Recognition: NEA may recognize and appreciate the contributions of outstanding employees, teams, or partners who have made significant contributions to NEA's success or the electricity sector in Nepal. Knowledge Sharing and Awareness Programs: NEA may organize workshops, seminars, or conferences to share knowledge and experiences related to the electricity sector. These events can focus on technological advancements, energy policies, renewable energy promotion.

what we do

The Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) is responsible for several key functions related to the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity in Nepal. Here are some of the primary tasks and activities undertaken by NEA:Electricity Generation: NEA is involved in the generation of electricity through its own hydropower projects and thermal power plants.Transmission and Grid Operation: NEA operates and maintains the national transmission grid, which connects power plants to substations and major load centers across the country.
